Product Name

Cotton Rich

Recycled Knitting Yarn

Our recycled knitting yarns are cotton rich and available in a wide range of colours with 115 plus shades. They are available in coarse and fine counts and in single ply and double ply options.

JK knitting yarns are available in different composition of blends, including: Cotton, Cotton and Polyester, Cotton and Viscose, Cotton Poly and Viscose, Cotton Poly and Wool Blends. Our regenerated knit yarns are suitable for running on any types of knitting machines like circular, flat or jacquard for making single jersey, fleece, french terry, pique, rib and interlock fabrics.
