About Us

jk yarns Textile started from the hometown of textiles in Cangnan, Zhejiang in 1990. jk yarns Textile has engaged in the yarns industry for more than 30 years. Our main products are wovening yarn / knitting yarn / denim yarn/sock yarn/ gloves yarn / mop yarn / cotton rope yarn / carpet yarn, etc. In addition, jk yarns Textile has a variety of yarns, including cotton/TC/CVC/CR/CTR, etc., and the count covers from Ne0.2 to Ne60. The group takes "integrity, excellent quality, strict management" as its business philosophy and regards quality as the first place. It has won the great reputation in the market and got the trust of our customers. The products are exported to Europe, America, Southeast Asia and other countries.
